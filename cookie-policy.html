1. What Are Cookies

Cookies are small text files placed on your device when you visit a website. They help websites function properly, remember your preferences, and provide information to website owners about how visitors use their site.

2. Types of Cookies We Use

Cookie Type Purpose Duration
Essential Required for the website to function properly. Cannot be disabled. Session
Analytics Help us understand how visitors interact with our website by collecting anonymous usage data. Up to 2 years
Functional Remember your preferences and settings to improve your experience. Up to 1 year

3. Specific Cookies Used

Currently, www.priven.io is a static informational website. We use minimal cookies primarily for essential website functionality. We do not currently use advertising or tracking cookies.

4. Third-Party Cookies

5. Managing Cookies

You can control and manage cookies in several ways:

  • Browser settings: Most browsers allow you to refuse or delete cookies through their settings.
  • Opt-out tools: You can use browser extensions to block tracking cookies.

Please note that disabling certain cookies may affect the functionality of our website.
