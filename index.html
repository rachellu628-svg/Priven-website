About

Who We Are

Priven is a programmatic ad exchange built on the principle that clean traffic and transparent supply chains are not optional — they are the foundation of sustainable programmatic growth.

With deep expertise in mobile in-app advertising and the global programmatic ecosystem, we connect DSPs and publishers through a marketplace where every impression is verified, every node is accountable, and every partner is treated as a long-term relationship.

We are a boutique exchange — small by design, precise by intent. Our focus is mobile in-app inventory across global markets, with a particular emphasis on quality over volume.
